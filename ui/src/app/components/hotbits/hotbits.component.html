Hotbits (or true random numbers derived from a natural quantum process) are the fuel for analysis and broadcasting.

Currently hotbits are not collected automatically. Therefore you need to trigger it manually.

WebCam Hotbits

Point you WebCam to the ceiling or a neutral background. Avoid flickering lights or lava lamps, because the randomness should originate from the WebCam's own electronic noise. The quantum tunneling in the sensor delivers perfect true random numbers, which are not determined even if stored on a hard drive. But they will be determined by you, while you observe the analysis result. This is mind over matter in action!
